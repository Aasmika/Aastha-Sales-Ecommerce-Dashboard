This project presents an end-to-end ecommerce sales analysis using Power BI, focusing on sales trends, customer behavior, and product profitability.

Dashboard Features

KPI Cards: Overall sales amount, profit, quantity sold, and average order value

Geo Analysis: State-wise sales contribution

Product Insights: Category and sub-category breakdown

Time Series: Monthly profit visualization to track business growth

Customer Insights: Top contributors by purchase amount

Payment Mode Analysis: Understanding customer payment preferences

Purpose

The goal of this dashboard is to deliver clear, data-driven insights that help businesses:

Identify high-value customers

Understand product performance

Optimize sales strategy

Monitor overall business health
